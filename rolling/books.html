Books

Management Information System (MIS)

This book describe the concepts of what is a management information system. This book gives a sense of how information systems have evolved, been refined and adapted as new emerging technologies and other IT-based management related factors which influenced the use of information systems to leverage the business process. Published By Pareen Publication, Mumbai.

Graphical User Interface

This book represent to a compilation of fundamental principles for designing user interfaces, which have been drawn from various books on interface design, as well as my own experience. Most of these principles can be applied to either command-line or graphical environments. This book also covers the practical aspects of GUI application using VB and VC++.This book is reflection of the significant progress that has been made in this field in just a past decade. Published By Pareen Publication, Mumbai.

Human Machine Interaction

This book describes the study of interactions between humans and machines. Human Machine Interaction is a multidisciplinary field with contributions from Human-Computer interaction (HCI), Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Humanoid robots and exoskeleton control.Published By Wiley Publications,2015

Digital forensics

Digital forensics (sometimes known as digital forensic science) is a branch of forensic science encompassing the recovery and investigation of material found in digital devices, often in relation to computer crime.[1][2] The term digital forensics was originally used as a synonym for computer forensics but has expanded to cover investigation of all devices capable of storing digital data.Published By Wiley Publications,2015
